Synapse is the reference Matrix homeserver maintained by Element, powering federated, end-to-end encrypted real-time communications including messaging, VoIP, and file sharing. It underpins deployments across European governments and healthcare, including Germany's TI-Messenger and France's Tchap national messaging platform.
