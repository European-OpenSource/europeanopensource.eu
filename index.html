Why Join European OpenSource?

Adding your open-source project to European OpenSource means becoming part of a unique ecosystem that offers concrete advantages for your project and the community.

Increased Visibility

Showcase your project to a targeted European audience of developers, companies, and institutions actively looking for innovative solutions.

Growing Community

Connect with like-minded developers and organizations who share your values of transparency, collaboration, and digital sovereignty.

Collaboration Opportunities

Foster partnerships and collaborations with other European projects, creating synergies that amplify innovation and impact.

Digital Sovereignty

Contribute to building a more independent and secure European digital infrastructure, reducing dependency on external solutions.

Sustainable Future

Be part of an ethical approach to technology that prioritizes sustainability, accessibility, and positive social impact.

Quality Recognition

Gain recognition as a high-quality, European-vetted project that meets strict standards for innovation, documentation, and community value.

How the submission process works

  1. Submit Project

    Fill out the form with all the necessary information. The more details you provide, the faster your project will be evaluated.

  2. Evaluation

    Our team ensures the project meets all the criteria to be included in the European OpenSource platform.

  3. Publication

    Once approved, your project is added to the GitHub repository and published on the platform. You will be notified via email.

Eligibility Criteria
