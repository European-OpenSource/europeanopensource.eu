Manifesto

The first platform for the European tech ecosystem

European OpenSource is the first platform connecting developers, companies, and institutions to share code, knowledge, and innovative ideas. Our goal is to transform fragmentation into tangible collaboration, promoting software reuse and generating a positive social impact.

Our mission is to build an open-source ecosystem that represents a common good: transparent, accessible, and open to everyone. A sovereign Europe leading global innovation with ethics and inclusivity.

Eligibility Criteria

Adding your open-source project to European OpenSource means becoming part of a unique ecosystem. Increase your project's visibility and contribute to building a more collaborative, transparent, and sustainable technological future.

European Affiliation
Your company is based in an EU country
Non-EU company with at least 2/3 founders being European citizens
You are a developer or creator with EU citizenship

How the Submission Process Works

  1. Submit the Project

    Fill out the form with all the necessary information. The more details you provide, the faster your project will be evaluated.

  2. Evaluation

    Our team ensures the project meets all the criteria to be included in the European OpenSource platform.

  3. Publication

    Once approved, your project is added to the GitHub repository and published on the platform. You will be notified via email.
